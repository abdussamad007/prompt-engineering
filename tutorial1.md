## Write a prompt to perform the task by following TCREI prompting framework: task, context, reference, evaluate, iterate.
Think of a situation from your work or personal life where you need help planning and tracking a timeline in a table. 
Maybe it’s a timeline for a product launch, highlighting key milestones like research, gathering resources, 
creation, and testing. Perhaps you're onboarding a new employee and need a timeline to guide their paperwork,
training sessions, and team introductions. Or are you organizing a conference and require a timeline detailing
venue booking, speaker coordination, and attendee registration? Maybe you’re looking for a more personal project, 
like a timeline for creating a beautiful garden, with time built in for planting seasons, maintenance tasks, 
and the joy of watching your flowers bloom.

* Specific dates for the timeline, like a deadline to submit an application.
* Certain limitations in availability, like employee vacations or supply chain disruptions.
* Limits to how often something can occur in the timeline, such as a daily task or weekly meeting.

```
The TCREI Prompt
Task:
Create a detailed product launch timeline organized in a Markdown table.
The table must include columns for "Phase," "Task Name," "Duration," "Key Milestone/Deliverable," and "Priority Level."
Context:
I am launching a new eco-friendly skincare line in six months.
I need to track everything from the initial R&D phase to the final digital marketing push.
 The timeline needs to account for potential supply chain delays and include specific windows
 for product testing and dermatological certification.
Role:
Act as an Expert Project Manager with 10+ years of experience in retail product launches.
Your tone should be organized, strategic, and proactive, identifying potential bottlenecks before they happen.
Exemplar:
For example, under the "Research" phase, a task might be "Sourcing Sustainable Packaging," lasting 3 weeks,
with the milestone being "Supplier Contract Signed," and a "High" priority.
Iteration:
Once you provide the initial table, I will ask you to drill down into the "Marketing & Awareness" phase specifically.
For now, please focus on a high-level overview of the entire 6-month cycle.
